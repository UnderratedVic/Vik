The Journey

I've been on a challenging journey of self-discovery, healing, and faith. I went through a long period of depression, and it affected my ability to remember much from that time. During those years, I had some complex relationships, but everything changed when I found Christ. That experience transformed me, and now I've dedicated myself to spreading love without limits. My faith is at the center of my life, and it’s through my walk with Christ that I find comfort, hope, and purpose.

Even though I feel deeply connected to God, I often feel isolated and long for someone to talk to, both about my spiritual life and personal struggles. I find it hard to open up, but when I’m alone, I can express myself easily through poetry. It’s how I make sense of everything. In conversations with friends, or when I’m talking about God, the words flow naturally, and I feel alive. But outside those moments, doubts creep in, and I sometimes struggle to fully share my heart.

I’m still learning and growing, and I want to be the best I can be for myself and those around me, with honesty, integrity, confidence, and respect. Through all of this, I’ve stayed resilient, determined to spread the love and joy I’ve found in Christ.
